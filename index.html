Designing Education for Justice (DEFJ) provides opportunities & tools for educators and education professionals to engage in reflection and action towards equity & justice.

We define justice & equity in education as that which acknowledges and critiques power & oppression, prioritizes marginalized & underrepresented communities and transforms structures maintaining inequity and oppression.
