Design Events

Session Location About
Diversity & Inclusion 101: What do all these terms mean & why are they important to me? Zoom platform In this session we discuss key terms used in higher education diversity culture like anti-racism, equity, justice, underrepresented, BIPOC. We also discuss its significance & application of concepts in teaching practice & classroom environments
Inclusive Pedagogies Zoom platform : We review & discuss principles of several models of inclusive education and pedagogy. We also discuss the relevance of these models within our disciplinary classrooms.
Designing for Equity – Introduction In-person TBD We discuss the main ideas of inclusive & critical pedagogy as a way to frame equity in education. We also discuss what this might look like in practice & ways we can adapt these principles for our own classroom. This session provides space & support for participants to brainstorm ideas & identify one unit in their curriculum they can adapt using principles of critical pedagogy and transform to create a more equitable & inclusive classroom.
